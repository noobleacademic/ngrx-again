Dear teachers & parents,

REJOICE

You can now focus on what is most important - the quality of our children's education.

Nooble is an intelligent, education technology offering, crafted to enhance the quality of education, by providing tools that augment facilitation & learning.

Introducing a super-simplified fees life cycle management

We nailed the fees madness

Institutions

Create multiple types of fees for specific student categories in just a few clicks, from your own secure domain. Monitor its status, follow-up and view comprehensive reports for detailed analyses.

Parents

No more queues for fee payments, issuance of cheques or missed payments. Get fees notifications on your phone and pay seamlessly through a secured gateway. Keep track of all your fee payments from your comprehensive dashboard.

Time is Money

For institutions, Nooble simplifies the fee creation and collection process, saving precious time for teachers and administrative staff.

For parents, it saves time and removes the hassles of standing in ATM queues, issuing cheques or visiting the school to pay fees.

Secure & Exclusive

Get a cloud-hosted solution for your institution on your own custom domain, to manage all your fee transactions.

With state-of-the-art security features in place, both parents & the management can now be at peace regarding the safety of their data & transactions.

Mobile and Realtime

Institutions can now create fee structures in just a few clicks, thanks to the extremely user-friendly interface.

Fees notifications will also reach parents in realtime, enabling them to pay with just a click from their registered mobile phones or desktops.

Awesome Analytics

Stay updated on not just all due and paid amounts, but also their category distributions through our comprehensive analytics.

Dashboards are not just for businesses. As a parent, it helps to know where your hard-earned money is going.Our analytics for parents do just that. Beautifully.
